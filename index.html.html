Email Setup
Connect a real address so pickup notices send automatically
💡 How this works: This app sends email through EmailJS, a free service that lets a web page send mail without a server. Create a free account at emailjs.com, connect your Gmail/Outlook, then paste the three values below. Takes about 5 minutes, one time only.
📧 EmailJS Credentials
Found under Email Services (Service ID), Email Templates (Template ID), and Account → General (Public Key) in your EmailJS dashboard.
📝 Required Template Variables

When creating your template in EmailJS, use these exact variable names so this app can fill them in:

{{to_email}}
Client's email address
{{to_name}}
Client's name
{{subject}}
Email subject line
{{message}}
Full email body
